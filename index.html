Safe, Secure & Private

Your privacy is our top priority. Here‚Äôs why you can trust StatusSphere.

Works Offline

The app does not require an internet connection and cannot send any of your data or files online.

Local Access Only

It only reads temporary files from the specific WhatsApp status folders on your device.

No Data Collected

The app has no analytics or trackers. It does not collect or store any of your personal information.

Modern Android versions have strict security rules. The WhatsApp status folder is hidden in a protected system area. The "All Files Access" permission is the only way for the app to automatically find these folders without forcing you to manually select them. The app uses this permission only to read the `.Statuses` folders and for nothing else.
